# Run Your First Activity

The best way to understand ClaryNext is to experience it. This guide walks you through running your first activity.

## What is an Activity?

An activity is a guided experience that helps you apply knowledge through action. Activities break down complex wisdom into actionable steps, with optional AI assistance and mentor support.

**Activities are designed to:**
- Be available exactly when you need them
- Guide you through real situations in real-time
- Document your journey for later reflection
- Build lasting habits through repetition

## Finding an Activity

### From Marketplace

1. Click **Marketplace** in the navigation
2. Browse categories or search for a topic
3. Look for activities marked as **Free** to try first
4. Click an activity to view its details

### From Dashboard

Activities shared with you appear in **My Activities**.

### Using AI to Create One

1. Navigate to the **Design** section
2. Use Sketchbook AI to describe what you want to work on
3. Get a personalized activity created for you

## Starting an Activity

1. Click **Run** on an activity
2. Choose **Start New** or **Resume** existing engagement
3. Work through each step

![Running an Activity](/img/screenshots/running-activity.gif)

## During the Activity

### Working Through Steps

- **Text steps** — Read content, absorb information
- **Input steps** — Enter your thoughts and responses
- **Reflection steps** — Think deeply about questions
- **AI steps** — Get personalized feedback based on your inputs

### Saving Take-Aways

Click the **⋮ menu** on any text field to:
- **Add as action item** — Save as a task for later
- **Copy to insights** — Save as a learning

The **Take-Aways panel** shows your saved items.

## Completing the Activity

When finished:
1. **Engagement Completed** dialog shows your take-aways
2. Review your action items and insights
3. Add an optional **epilogue** reflection
4. Click **Continue** to return to dashboard

Your take-aways appear in your **Workspace** for ongoing tracking.

## Tips for Success

- **Choose the right moment** — Find a time when you won't be rushed
- **Be honest** — Authentic responses lead to better insights
- **Take your time** — Most activities have no timer
- **Save important content** — Use take-aways to capture key learnings
