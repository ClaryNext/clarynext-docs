# What is ClaryNext?

**ClaryNext** is the Actionable Wisdom Exchange Platform. We bridge the knowing-doing gap by transforming abstract wisdom into guided activities and on-demand mentoring.

> **"Abstract wisdom is potential; execution in context creates immediate value."**

## How It Works

1. **Recognize** — Identify the pattern you want to change
2. **Get** — Access an expert-created activity
3. **Practice** — Receive guided practice in real situations
4. **Build** — Form habits through repetition
5. **Master** — Make it your new default behavior

## Who Is It For?

| User Type | What You Do |
|-----------|-------------|
| **End Users** | Run activities for personal development |
| **Authors** | Create guided activities |
| **Experts** | Guide mentees through activities |
| **Vendors** | Sell activities in the marketplace |
| **Organizations** | Deploy training programs |

## Key Features

- **Activities** — Step-by-step guided experiences with AI assistance
- **Marketplace** — Discover and purchase activities from experts
- **Workspace** — Track action items and insights from your activities
- **AI Assistance** — Get help breaking down tasks and generating content
- **Mentoring** — Connect with experts for real-time guidance

## The Platform

### For Users

Run interactive activities that guide you through real-world situations. Capture insights and action items as you go, then track them in your personal workspace.

### For Experts

Create activities from your methodology using our visual designer or AI-powered Sketchbook. Support clients with real-time mentoring sessions and track their progress.

### For Organizations

Deploy training programs, manage teams, and track engagement across your organization with detailed analytics.
