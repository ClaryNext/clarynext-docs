# Registration & Login

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [app.clarynext.com](https://app.clarynext.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Register with email, Google, or Microsoft
4. Complete your profile (name, language, timezone)
5. Verify your email if using email registration

## Signing In

1. Go to [app.clarynext.com](https://app.clarynext.com)
2. Click **Login**
3. Enter credentials or use social login

## Password Recovery

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Click the reset link in your email
4. Create a new password

## Organization Accounts

If joining through an organization:
1. Check email for invitation
2. Click the invitation link
3. Create account or sign in
