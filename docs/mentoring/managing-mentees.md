# Managing Mentees

The Mentees section is your hub for managing client relationships.

## Adding Mentees

### By Invitation

1. Click **Add Mentee**
2. Enter mentee's email
3. Optionally assign activities
4. Click **Send Invitation**

### From Existing User

Search by email or name to connect with existing users.

## Mentee Profiles

Click on any mentee to see:

- **Overview** — Contact info, status, progress
- **Activities** — Assigned activities and progress
- **Sessions** — Upcoming and past sessions
- **Notes** — Your observations

## Organizing Mentees

### Groups

Create groups for cohorts or programs:
1. Go to **Mentees > Groups**
2. Click **New Group**
3. Add mentees

### Tags

Apply tags for filtering and organization.

## Working with Mentees

### Assigning Activities

1. Open mentee profile
2. Go to **Activities** tab
3. Click **Assign Activity**
4. Select activities

### Reviewing Responses

See mentee responses by selecting an activity in their profile.

### Adding Notes

Document observations with private or shared notes.
