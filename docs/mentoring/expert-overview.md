# Expert & Mentor Overview

As a mentor on ClaryNext, you can scale your expertise and support clients with guided activities.

## What Experts Can Do

- **Create Activities** — Transform your methodology into interactive experiences
- **Guide Mentees** — Support clients and see their progress
- **Sell in Marketplace** — Publish activities for purchase
- **Manage Practice** — Track clients and schedule sessions

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Upgrade Your Account

1. Go to **Account > Upgrade**
2. Select Expert or Author plan

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile > Expert Settings**
2. Add credentials and bio
3. Set your availability

### Step 3: Create Your First Activity

1. Navigate to **Design**
2. Click **Create New Activity**
3. Use the designer or Sketchbook AI

### Step 4: Add Mentees

1. Go to **Mentees**
2. Click **Add Mentee**
3. Send invitation email

### Step 5: Share Activities

1. Open your activity
2. Click **Share**
3. Select your mentee(s)

## Expert Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Active Mentees and their status
- Recent activity progress
- Upcoming sessions
- Messages
