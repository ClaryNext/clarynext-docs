# Chat & Messaging

## Accessing Chat

Click the chat icon in the navigation to open the chat panel.

## Conversation Types

- **Mentor Conversations** — Communicate with your mentors
- **Support** — Contact ClaryNext support
- **System Messages** — Automated notifications

## Sending Messages

1. Open a conversation
2. Type your message
3. Press Enter or click Send

You can attach files and images to messages.

## Notifications

Configure message notifications at **Settings > Notifications**:
- In-app notifications
- Email notifications
- Push notifications
