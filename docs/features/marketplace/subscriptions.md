# Subscriptions

## Plans Overview

| Plan | Features |
|------|----------|
| **Free** | Access to free activities, limited runs |
| **Basic** | Unlimited basic activities, full workspace |
| **Premium** | Premium library, AI credits, priority support |
| **Author** | Authoring tools, publishing, analytics |

## Managing Your Subscription

### View or Change

1. Go to **Account > Subscription**
2. View current plan
3. Upgrade or downgrade

### Billing

- Monthly or annual billing
- Update payment at **Account > Billing**
- View invoices and history

### Cancelling

1. Go to **Account > Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Access continues until period ends

## Organization Plans

For teams:
- Multiple user seats
- Centralized billing
- Admin controls
