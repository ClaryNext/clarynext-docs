# Purchasing Activities

## Pricing Models

- **One-Time Purchase** — Pay once, access forever
- **Credits** — Pre-purchased access tokens
- **Subscriptions** — Ongoing access plans

## Making a Purchase

1. Find an activity in the marketplace
2. Click **Purchase** or **Buy Now**
3. Apply any discount codes
4. Select payment method
5. Complete purchase

## Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Cards
- PayPal (where available)
- Activity Credits
- Organization billing

## After Purchase

- Activity appears in **My Activities**
- Start immediately
- Email receipt sent

## Managing Purchases

View purchase history at **Account > Purchases**.
