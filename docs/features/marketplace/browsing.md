# Browsing the Marketplace

The Marketplace is where you discover activities created by experts, coaches, and organizations.

![ClaryNext Marketplace](/img/screenshots/marketplace.gif)

## Accessing the Marketplace

Click **Marketplace** in the main navigation.

## Marketplace Layout

### Featured Section

At the top, you'll find:
- **Editor's Picks** — Hand-selected quality activities
- **New & Notable** — Recently published content
- **Trending** — Popular with other users

### Categories

Activities are organized by topic:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Communication & Relationships** | Connecting with others, difficult conversations |
| **Emotional Intelligence** | Managing emotions, stress, reactions |
| **Productivity & Focus** | Getting things done, decision-making |
| **Leadership & Management** | Leading teams, developing others |
| **Life Transitions** | Navigating change, career shifts |

### Activity Cards

Each card shows:
- Title and author
- Rating and price
- Brief description
- Tags

## Searching

Use the search bar to find activities by keyword:
- "difficult conversations"
- "stop procrastinating"
- "building habits"
- "career transition"

## Filtering Results

Filter by:
- **Category** — Topic area
- **Price** — Free or paid
- **Rating** — User ratings
- **Duration** — Activity length
- **Format** — Self-paced, expert-guided, series

## Viewing Activity Details

Click any card to see:
- Full description and learning outcomes
- Types of components included
- User reviews and ratings
- Author profile and credentials

## Saving for Later

- **Favorites** — Click the heart icon to save
- **Collections** — Organize activities into groups

## Free Activities

Many activities are completely free:
- Sample experiences
- Introductory content
- Community contributions
