# Insights

Insights are key learnings and reflections captured from your activities.

## Accessing Insights

Go to **My Workspace** → **Insights** tab.

## Capturing Insights

### During Activities

1. Click **⋮** on any text field
2. Select **Copy to insights**
3. The content is saved to your Take-Aways panel

### Manually

Click **+ Add** in the Take-Aways panel to write your own insight.

## Viewing Insights

Each insight shows:
- The content you saved
- Source activity link
- Date captured

## Using Insights

Review your insights to:
- Remember important realizations
- Track patterns over time
- Share with mentors
- Inform future decisions

## Organizing Insights

- Tag insights for easy filtering
- Group by activity or theme
- Search across all insights
