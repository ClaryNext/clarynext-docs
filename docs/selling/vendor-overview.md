# Vendor Overview

As a vendor, you can monetize your expertise by selling activities in the marketplace.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Upgrade to Vendor Plan

1. Go to **Account > Subscription**
2. Select plan with vendor features

### Step 2: Complete Vendor Profile

1. Go to **Profile > Vendor Settings**
2. Add bio, credentials, photo
3. Set up payment details

### Step 3: Create Your First Product

1. Create an activity
2. Configure pricing
3. Publish to marketplace

## Commerce Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Revenue summary
- Sales count
- Active products
- Analytics

## What You Can Sell

- **Individual Activities** — Single activities
- **Activity Bundles** — Collections of activities
- **Services** — Mentoring sessions, consulting

## Revenue Model

1. Customer purchases your product
2. ClaryNext processes payment and takes commission
3. Your earnings accumulate
4. Monthly payouts to your account
